2-storey villa surrounded by a large garden with lemon and olive trees, with great views of the sea, located in a very quiet location, halfway between the Marina beach and the medieval town of Pisciotta.  
  
The space  
The main apartment is very large (about 150sqm.) and is located at the first floor of the villa. It consists of an entrance area, a large living room with couches, television and a dining table, a kitchen, three bedrooms and three toilets (one bedroom has the toilet). The living area is surrounded by a large balcony where you can have dinner outdoor. Part of the balcony is covered with a portico, in order to enjoy the breeze in the shade). The house is located in the area of 'Piano di Mare - Stazione Vecchia', at just 10-mins walking distance or 1-km car ride from from the Marina of Pisciotta. The area is very quiet and silent, perfect for cooling down and relaxing after a long day at the beach.  
  
Guest access  
The apartment disposes of an independent access on the first floor, from the main facade of the building.
